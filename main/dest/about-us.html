About TOP SEO

TOP SEO was created on our CEO Ken Wisnefski’s core principles of actually providing a service that helps clients succeed.
Since we began as TOP SEO in 2008, we have continued to learn and refine our process as the industry evolves. Along the way we developed advanced quality control and reporting, internal and external continuing education for our staff and a cutting edge content development and marketing team.

With over 100 dedicated employees, TOP SEO has amongst the largest pool of Internet marketing professionals you will find at an agency. TOP SEO is led by an experienced management team, which provides strategic direction and support for the company. Our management team’s hands-on approach has led to TOP SEO becoming a multiple recipient of numerous “Best Places to Work” awards in the Philadelphia region.
